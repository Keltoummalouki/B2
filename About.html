About JapArt Gallery

JapArt Gallery boasts one of the world's most extensive collections of Japanese antiques, offering a captivating array of artefacts that span centuries. From 13th-century samurai swords to intricately designed lacquer wares, ukiyo-e woodblock prints, and exquisite ceramics, our collection reflects the richness of Japan’s artistic heritage. Since our founding in 2020, we have been proudly located in Tokyo's prestigious antiques district, a global hub for collectors and enthusiasts alike. To learn more about our services, please visit our FAQ page.

About Us

We are dedicated to sharing the beauty of Japanese art with the world. Our carefully curated collection features exquisite prints, paintings, and calligraphy from master artists, each piece reflecting the rich cultural heritage and timeless elegance of Japan.
